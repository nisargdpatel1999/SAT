Parts of Speech:

Explains how a word is USED. For example nouns are people, places, or objects which can be used as reference triggers so people know what you are explaining. A word can have multiple parts of speech depending on how it is used.

Parts of Speech Meaning Types Examples
Verbs Actions or states of being
  • Conjugation
  • Regular/Irregular
  • Number/Person
  • Tense
  • Infinitive
 To be, to feel, to have, to go, to run
Nouns People, places, subjects, objects, and ideas
  • Peoper nouns-specific (Alex)
  • Common nouns-general (girl)
 Girl, bicycle, supervisor, notion, mark, press
Pronouns Replace Nouns
  • Subject Pronouns
  • Object Pronouns
  • Relative Pronouns
  • Reflexive Pronouns
 She, They, you, one, we, his, this, that, them, him, which, I, him
Preposition Help connect 2 nouns or pronouns to each other. Can help explain where or when a part of the sentence occurred
  • Prepositional phrases
 To, from, in, on, with, between, without, around, under, over, before, during, after, at, through, of, for, by, within, under, along, before, after, during, around, near, about
Adjectives Modify nouns and pronouns Large, pretty. The class was so boring. The stunning view had me breathless
Adverb Modify verb, adjectives, and other adverbs She smiled warmly at him. He received an exceedingly good grades on the test. He ran very quickly
Conjunction Indicate relationship or linkage between words, phrases, and clauses
  • Coordinating conjunctions
  • Subordinate conjunctions
 FANBOYS = For, And, Nor, But, Or, Yet, So.
Since, during, when, although, because, while.
Interjection Express emotions Hi, Bye, Well

Verbs:

Basics of Verbs-

Infinitive (basic form)- to be, to have, to seem, to go

Conjugated Verb (number + person)- NOT TENSE

To Jump

Singular Plural
1st Person(I) - Jump 1st Person(We) - Jump
2nd Person(You) - Jump 2nd Person(You) - Jump
3rd Person(He, She, It, One) - Jumps 3rd Person(They) - Jump

Irregular - Base of verb changes - To be, To have

Present- To Be

Singular Plural
1st Person(I) - am 1st Person(We) - are
2nd Person(You) - are 2nd Person(Youare
3rd Person(He, She, It, One) - is 3rd Person(They) - are

Irregular - Base of verb changes - To be, To have

Past- To Be

Singular Plural
1st Person(I) - was 1st Person(We) - were
2nd Person(You) - were 2nd Person(Youwere
3rd Person(He, She, It, One) - was 3rd Person(They) - were

Present- To Have

Singular Plural
1st Person(I) - have 1st Person(We) - have
2nd Person(You) - have 2nd Person(Youhave
3rd Person(He, She, It, One) - has 3rd Person(They) - have

Past- To Have

Singular Plural
1st Person(I) - had 1st Person(We) - had
2nd Person(You) - had 2nd Person(Youhad
3rd Person(He, She, It, One) - had 3rd Person(They) - had

Regular- Base of verb doesn't change - To jump, To play

Tense- Shows time when an action occured

  • Present: She speaks
  • Present Perfect: She has spoken
  • Past: She spoke
  • Past Perfect: She had spoken
  • Future: She will speak
  • Future Perfect: She will have spoken
  • Conditional: She would speak
  • Conditional Past: She would have spoken
